Codes and Standards

Helping to bridge the gap
between technology and
Codes and Standards

IntelliSIMS' staff contribute actively to the development of industry codes and standards through a combination of:

  • Voluntary participation on API and ISO committees and technical panels,
  • Engineering studies to demonstrate technology readiness for code implementation,
  • Leadership of JIPs to develop best-practice for future code adoption, and
  • Participation in industry knowledge dissemination workshops to share new or updated codes and standards with wider industry.

Some recent existing or pending publications that IntelliSIMS' staff have actively supported include:

  • API RP 2 A 22nd Ed.
  • API RP 2 SIM 1st Ed.
  • API RP 2 FB 1st Ed.
  • ISO 19902 2nd Ed.
