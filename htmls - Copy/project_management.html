Project management expertise that keeps results, budgets and schedules in line

iSIMS project managers and engineers have extensive experience in all aspects of onshore and offshore projects from major plants and refineries to onshore plants and pipelines to shallow inland and coastal waters and deep offshore fields. iSIMS provides project management and oversight for new-build platforms, facilities and pipelines, as well as maintenance and repair operations and salvage and abandonment programs. We also provide feasibility studies, assessment of existing assets, field development consultation, and costs estimating.

iSIMS engineering personnel bring a deep understanding of the relevant design codes and standards brought about by their active participation in the technical committees that draft and maintain these standards including API Sub-committee 2 where iSIMS is represented. This committee is responsible for the maintenance and update of API RP 2A applicable to the design of fixed offshore platforms. iSIMS is able to bring this understanding of code intent to the benefit of our customers with innovative design concepts based in knowledge and experience.
