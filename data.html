SIM
Data

The starting point of your
IM System

Management of the characteristic data that represents the structural system and changes to the structure and its condition with time.

Data is the foundation and starting point of any integrity management (IM) program or system. Data quality and completeness influences the effectiveness of your IM system.

Data can generally be categorized as either:

  • Characteristic Data-baseline data associated with the component or facility during the design phase (e.g. drawings, fabrication records & design codes)
  • Condition Data-operational data which is periodically updated to reflect the component's changing integrity status (e.g. inspection data, updates earthquake criteria, on-line monitoring data).

Let IntelliSIMS help you organize your IM data and get one step closer to implementing your IM program today.

Services include:

  • Data audits and reviews to enable you to identify and compile your key IM data
  • Client specific data management solutions to allow you to create and maintain your IM database
